Underwater Services

Practical diving support for boats, property, and recovered valuables

Each job begins with a clear discussion of the location, water conditions, access, depth, and recovery objective.

01

Hull Inspection & Cleaning

Visual inspection and cleaning of accessible underwater hull surfaces, running gear, intakes, props, and related components.

  • Routine hull-condition checks
  • Marine-growth removal
  • Propeller and running-gear inspection
  • Photo or video documentation when appropriate
02

Lost-Item Recovery

Targeted underwater searches for items lost from docks, boats, beaches, seawalls, and other accessible waterfront locations.

  • Keys and wallets
  • Phones and cameras
  • GoPros and fishing equipment
  • Personal and boat-related equipment
03

Light Salvage

Recovery and repositioning of manageable submerged objects when conditions and equipment requirements remain within the scope of light salvage.

  • Small equipment recovery
  • Lines, anchors, and dock hardware
  • Underwater object location
  • Job-specific recovery planning

Service Area

Serving two major boating regions

Hull & Recovery Diving serves customers throughout the Great Lakes region and the Florida Keys. Availability depends on location, season, weather, water conditions, travel requirements, and the scope of work.

Great Lakes Freshwater marinas, docks, lakes, rivers, and waterfront properties
Florida Keys Saltwater marinas, private docks, vessels, and nearshore recovery locations
Professional • Reliable • Safety Focused

Every dive is evaluated before entering the water.

Water visibility, current, access, depth, entanglement hazards, vessel traffic, contamination, weather, and required lifting capacity can affect whether a job can be completed safely.

Final service availability is determined after reviewing the specific job conditions.

About H&R Diving

Focused service for real underwater problems

Hull & Recovery Diving was established to provide straightforward, hands-on diving support for boat owners and waterfront customers. The company focuses on practical jobs where clear communication, careful planning, and dependable underwater work matter.

From inspecting a boat hull to searching for a phone dropped from a dock, each request receives a job-specific assessment rather than a one-size-fits-all response.
